  • Components providing thrust must be on the same line as the maneuver they are used for.
  • For reusable rockets & Ion Thrusters, use the (reuse) option after the first usage, to keep mass & cost accurate.
  • Additional non-thrusting components/mass should go on the line above their thruster(s) (with From/To blank).
  • To clear and start over, just refresh the page.
